Contract Negotiation FAQs

We use a proprietary valuation model that analyzes:

  • Performance Metrics: Position-specific statistics compared to league standards
  • Market Comparables: Recent contracts for similar players
  • League Economics: Club finances and spending patterns
  • Commercial Potential: Marketing value in target markets
  • Age Curve: Projected performance over contract duration

African transfers require special handling of:

  • Work Permits: Navigating complex visa requirements
  • Cultural Transitions: Preparing players for radical lifestyle changes
  • Payment Structures: Ensuring reliable payment channels
  • Third-Party Ownership: Resolving historical ownership issues
  • Medical Standards: Bridging healthcare expectation gaps

Our dispute resolution protocol includes:

  • Prevention: Clear contract language with defined metrics
  • Mediation: Informal resolution through relationship management
  • Legal Action: When necessary, we engage our sports law partners
  • Public Relations: Protecting your reputation during disputes
  • Exit Strategies: Negotiated termination when appropriate

Our transfer fee structure is:

  • Standard Rate: 10% of transfer fee (capped at $500k)
  • Performance Bonus: Additional 2-5% for fees above target
  • Youth Transfers: Reduced 5% rate for players under 21
  • Domestic Moves: Flat fee structure for local transfers
  • No Hidden Fees: Full transparency on all payments
