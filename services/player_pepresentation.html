Frequently Asked Questions

We evaluate athletes through our proprietary 4P Framework: Potential, Personality, Professionalism, and Perseverance. Our scouts assess technical ability while our psychologists evaluate mental resilience. We prioritize athletes who demonstrate both exceptional skill and the capacity for long-term development.

We employ a Future-Proof Negotiation strategy that considers not just current market value but projected career trajectory. Our contracts typically include:

  • Performance-based escalators
  • Image rights protections
  • Career transition options
  • Post-career benefits
  • Tax optimization structures

Our Cultural Transition Program includes:

  • Pre-move cultural orientation sessions
  • Language training tailored to sports terminology
  • Local mentor matching in destination countries
  • Regular check-ins with our transition specialists
  • Family support services for relocated athletes

Our standard representation fee is 10% of playing contracts and 20% of endorsement deals, with several important distinctions:

  • No upfront fees - we only succeed when you succeed
  • Reduced rates for youth athletes
  • Performance-based fee reductions after certain thresholds
  • Transparent accounting with quarterly reports
